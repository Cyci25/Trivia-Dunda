Frequently Asked Questions

What you'd want to know.

  • How do I sign up for the Trivia Dunda for Business for my team?

    We would love to collaborate with you to bring out the best version of your team. Please click here and our Trivia Dunda Master and Host shall reach out to you within 24 hours to plan a connect and meeting.

    We have affordable options to choose from starting from as low as 80,000KES (excl. tax)

  • What is Trivia Dunda Business?

      We are a Kenyan based company created by our co-founders united with a love for trivia and people. Our CEO and co-founder Alvin Wamocho loves curating trivia content and Klaire the Brand Titan is a formidable trivia host who loves bringing out the best in our clients. We create and curate unforgettable trivia events that re-energize our clients with laughter, pride, and a renewed sense of belonging and togetherness that is unmatched.

      We do this by creating a bespoke event for our clients and their employees and ask rounds of exhilarating trivia questions and team players participate to score for every question. Questions are displayed on our TV screens and results for each team’s question and their scores are cumulated on-screen.

      The winner of the event is declared on the screen at the end of the last question and shows the ranking of all the other teams.

      A ‘Trivia’ is a type of game in which players are asked questions about different topics and they must get as many correct answers as possible within a given time.

  • What kind of trivia questions will be asked?

    1. General Knowledge
    2. Science
    3. Entertainment
    4. Academia
    5. Sports
    6. Kenyanese (uniquely Kenyan questions that only Kenyans might know)
    7. East African

  • What is the structure of the questions?

    The questions are timed depending on the difficulty (harder questions have more time allocated). Maximum time that can be allocated to any question is 60 seconds. The questions are also scored differently depending on the difficulty (harder questions score more points).

    Scoring is based on the fastest to correctly answer a question. The faster you are in answering correctly, the higher you score. Not answering, results in scoring a zero.

  • What do I need on the day of the Trivia Dunda Business experience?

    Please ensure the whole team carries their mobile devices/laptops as you can use the internet for answers. Kindly ensure you have the best internet connection, i.e., WI-FI or Data bundles.

  • How will the Trivia Dunda Business experience be done?

    1. Teams will assemble at the designated area.
    2. Trivia master will commence the event by explain the house rules
    3. A test run will be done to give the participants a feel of the platform. Kindly note that this round is not scored.
    4. After the test run, the actual trivia starts moderated by our Trivia Dunda Master and Host
